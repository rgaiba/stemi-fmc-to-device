# Manuscript folder — T₂ − T₁ paper

Before editing analysis code, tables, figures, or manuscript text in this folder, read `ANALYTICAL_DECISIONS.md`. That file is the durable record of decisions about scope, data sources, Discussion language, and reporting standards for this paper and its planned matchable-reroute sequel.

In particular:

- The paper's scope is the access-side T₂ − T₁ measurement only. No matchable-reroute count, no D2B IQR table.
- No scraped AHA Mission: Lifeline data anywhere in this project.
- Discussion may make a direction claim citing the peer-reviewed registry literature (Menees et al., NEJM 2013); Discussion may not make a quantitative matchable-reroute claim from aggregate registry IQR.
- The sequel matchable-reroute paper is a registry paper (GWTG-CAD or NCDR CathPCI), not a Care Compare or scrape paper.
- Reporting standard: AHA Statistical Update conventions throughout.

Cross-reference: project-level memory entries `project_d2b_match_study.md` and `project_binding_constraint_framing.md` mirror these decisions.
